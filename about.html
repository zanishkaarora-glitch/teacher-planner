About This System

The Teacher's Content Planner bridges the gap between lesson preparation and student awareness. It lets educators digitize syllabus breakdowns and gives students a clear view of upcoming tests and assignments.

  • Efficiency: Reduce paper-based planning.
  • Transparency: Students can view schedules 24/7.
  • Persistence: Data remains saved even after closing the browser.
